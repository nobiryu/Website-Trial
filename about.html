About Us

WELCOME TO UNISIGHT

TNZ Technologies, Inc. dba Unisight Digital Products, Inc. has been a leader in the development of software for digital video surveillance systems since it was established in 1998. Unisight highly prioritizes client needs, customer-specified feature development and tailor-made SDK integration for front-end vendors. We have established outstanding manufacturer relationships and provide product flexibility to help your business attain a strong presence in the security industry.

As technology develops, so does Unisight's software visionaries who continue to dedicate their intuitive reasoning which allows them to produce the most versatile security solution available on the market today. Our platform is based on the H.264/H.265 codec which is utilized to offer exceptional video quality at a low storage rate. Specifically with the newest camera technologies and high MegaPixel count - we have adapted Bandwidth Management Technology to maintain the highest quality video stream possible based on the existing data infrastructure.

A progressive development team, combined with breakthrough technology, has created a stable platform fit for mission-critical applications. Unisight Standard, Professional and Enterprise versions have been developed from this platform to provide limitless configuration options. Strategic integrations with other respected technology partners (IP/MegaPixel manufacturers, Access Control, Alarms and other electronic management developers), have allowed us to offer our customers hundreds of alternatives to maximize their offerings. Our newest Enterprise release presents a global hybrid monitoring solution complete with remote access and configuration capabilities for security anywhere!
